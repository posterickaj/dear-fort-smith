We already have a prescription for both of these problems, and it's not even a new tax. Land Tax can act as a vacancy tax since it charges everyone, including vacant landlords, equally! Land Tax does the job of impact fees since it scales with the amount of infrastructure a parcel consumes, as it consumes it, rather than a one-time startup fee that gets baked into housing prices!

We can remove property tax and sales tax altogether and replace them with a raised land tax. The result is that everyone will pay for what they themselves consume, without subsidizing the vacant lot next door, and crucially: keeping the wealth that they themselves create, in full.
