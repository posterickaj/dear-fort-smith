(1) **Education** - make sure people know how to use the road safely

(2) **Enforcement** - make sure the laws of the road are in place and applied to everyone

(3) **Engineering** - make sure the roads are designed to prompt the behavior we want from road users

Most of us know and care about the first two, but the third is equally important. Wide lanes signal to drivers that they may speed. Slip lanes invite drivers to speed up towards crosswalks and look away at the same time. Driveways on arterials introduce points of conflict.

This is why it is important that citizens know how much the 2026 Streets Masterplan will affect safety (and economics) of Fort Smith for generations to come.
