Dear Fort Smith,

Here is an early example of incrementalism. Your residents were debating whether to pave some roads. The opinion on the right is from Dr. J McCorkle, whose son William was Captain of the Rex Wheelmen, which was Fort Smith's chapter of the Good Roads League.  
William McCorkle was one of the "Three Wills" along with US Representative William B. Cravens and Will Ayers, who first paved our high street, Garrison Avenue, with bricks.
Dave Mayo (opinion on the left) won this debate, and we continue to follow his model today.
