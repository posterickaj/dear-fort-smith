Dear Fort Smith,

Save money by making roads safe in the design stage. 
The 2026 streets master plan needs oversight from small business owners, motorcyclists, transit users, joggers, dog walkers, and anyone else who lives the sorts of experiences that don't get taught in engineering classes.
