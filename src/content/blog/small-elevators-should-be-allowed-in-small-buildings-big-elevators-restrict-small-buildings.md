---
title: Big elevators restrict small buildings.
description: >
  Elevators are essential for buildings over 3 stories, but the building code
  requires they be built to accommodate stretchers, meaning that elevators are
  large and expensive, even in small, cute buildings.

  As a result, small, cute buildings don't get built anymore.

  Small buildings (6 stories and under) should be allowed to install smaller elevators if they have some other specialized emergency equipment that can handle emergency evacuations.

  If it's good enough for the Fort Smith National Historic Site (bottom picture), it's good enough for me.
